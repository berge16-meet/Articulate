ARTiculate is a social platform where people can upload and paint pictures regarding recent political events. The idea is to create a healthy discussion on the political subjects as posted in pictures. Thus we aim to create an objective platform in which various perspectives will be heard and presented. The choice of art was not random, it was chosen as a source of attraction to our website, as well as a universal element of communication, with no boundaries of nationality, language or anything else.

Our Vision


We envision a world where all voices are heard in order to build a society that is self-aware, exposed, and understands all perspectives.

Our Mission


Our mission is to create a circle of communication, established on the common ground of art, fulfilling tolerance and understanding towards all of its members.
